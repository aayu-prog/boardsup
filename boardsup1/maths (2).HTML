Board Student Rules and Regulations

Welcome, Board Students!

To ensure a productive and safe environment, please follow these rules:

General Rules

  • Respect fellow students, teachers, and staff.
  • Attend classes regularly and punctually.
  • Follow the dress code.
  • Keep your belongings organized and secure.

Academic Rules

  • Complete assignments on time.
  • Prepare for exams and assessments.
  • Seek help from teachers when needed.
  • Maintain honesty and integrity in academic work.

Disciplinary Rules

  • No bullying, harassment, or physical violence.
  • No use of electronic devices during classes.
  • Consequences for misconduct will be enforced.
